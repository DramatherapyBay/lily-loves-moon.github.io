Dramatherapy Bay Limited

Unlike traditional talk therapy, dramatherapy engages the whole person – mind, body, and spirit – creating opportunities for profound healing and self-discovery. Through the safe medium of creative play and storytelling, individuals can explore difficult emotions and develop greater self-awareness.

與傳統的談話治療不同，戲劇治療關注的是全人——包括思想、身體和精神。透過創意遊戲和故事敘說的安全媒介，個人可以探索困難的情緒並培養自我意識。

This innovative therapeutic approach allows clients to safely explore their emotions and relationships through techniques such as role-play, improvisation, storytelling, and creative movement. The process is conducted by licensed professionals.

這種創新的治療方法讓個案能夠透過角色扮演、即興創作、故事敘說和創意律動，安全地探索情緒與關係。過程由專業人士帶領。
