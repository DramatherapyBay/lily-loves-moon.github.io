Therapy Approach Structure Time Frame Suitable For Home Practice
Dramatherapy Creative, experiential therapy providing psychological distance to explore body image and emotions. Talking therapy, storytelling, role-play, movement. Participation optional. Usually weekly, several months. Anorexia, neurodivergence (ASD/ADHD), those finding direct discussion triggering. Reflection, noticing patterns, optional creative practice.
CBT-informed Structured, practical strategies integrated under a dramatherapy framework. Goal-focused. Assessment, goal setting, skill practice, progress review. Typically 10 sessions standard. Bulimia, Binge Eating Disorder, motivated clients ready for active change. Monitoring thoughts, practicing coping strategies, adjusting routines.
Guided Self-Help Evidence-informed exercises supported by therapist guidance. Short therapy sessions combined with self-help materials. Typically 8–12 sessions. Binge Eating, emotional eating, milder eating difficulties. Requires independent work. Completing exercises, monitoring thoughts, self-compassion activities.

Our focus is on developing a healthy, sustainable relationship with food, body, and self. While some clients may notice changes in weight as they recover, we do not promise or promote weight-loss outcomes.
