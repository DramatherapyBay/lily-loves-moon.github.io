DRAMATHERAPY
Approach

Creative, experiential therapy providing psychological distance to explore body image and emotions.

Structure

Talking, storytelling, role-play, movement. Participation is always optional.

Time Frame

Usually weekly, over several months.

Suitable For

Anorexia Nervosa, neurodivergence, or those who find direct discussion triggering.

Home Tasks

Reflection, noticing patterns, grounding exercises.

CBT-INFORMED
Approach

Structured strategies integrated under a dramatherapy framework to reduce unhelpful patterns.

Structure

Goal-focused. Assessment, goal setting, skill practice, and progress review.

Time Frame

Typically 10 sessions standard.

Suitable For

Bulimia Nervosa, Binge Eating, and motivated clients ready for active change.

Home Tasks

Monitoring thoughts, adjusting routines, practicing coping tools.

GUIDED SELF-HELP
Approach

Evidence-informed self-help exercises supported by therapist guidance.

Structure

Short therapy sessions combined with self-help materials and guidance.

Time Frame

Typically 8–12 sessions.

Suitable For

Binge Eating, emotional eating, or early/milder eating difficulties.

Home Tasks

Completing exercises, self-compassion activities, monitoring patterns.

"Our focus is on developing a healthy, sustainable relationship with food, body, and self. While some clients may notice changes in weight as they recover, we do not promise or promote weight-loss outcomes."
