Meet Lily Lau

HCPC registered Arts Therapist (Dramatherapist)

Professional Background

Originally from Hong Kong, Lily brings a multicultural perspective to her practice as a British registered dramatherapist. She uses drama and creativity to support emotional and psychological well-being, with a particular passion for working with diverse populations and complex mental health needs.

Lily's approach is grounded in empathy, cultural sensitivity, and respect for each individual's unique journey toward healing.

Therapeutic Philosophy

"Healing isn't about fixing what's broken—it's about awakening what's possible. Together, we'll use the transformative power of creativity to help you move from surviving to thriving."

Specialised ED Treatment

A Compassionate Approach to Weight and Wellbeing

While many dieting programmes focus on short-term weight loss, our service is different. We understand that some people may have a wish to lose weight, however our work centres on helping you develop a healthy, sustainable and compassionate relationship with food, eating and your body.

As eating patterns stabilise and health-behaviours improve, some people may naturally notice changes in weight over time — however we do not promise or promote weight-loss outcomes. Our priority is your emotional, psychological and physical wellbeing.
