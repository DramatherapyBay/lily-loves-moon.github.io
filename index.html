What is Dramatherapy?

Dramatherapy is a specialized form of psychotherapy that harnesses the power of drama, theater, and creative expression to promote healing and personal growth.

A Holistic Approach to Healing

Unlike traditional talk therapy, dramatherapy engages the whole person – mind, body, and spirit – creating opportunities for profound healing and self-discovery. Through role-play, improvisation, storytelling, and creative movement, individuals can safely explore emotions and relationships.

Evidence-Based Practice

Dramatherapy is recognized by the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC) and is used worldwide across clinical and community settings.
