What is Dramatherapy?

Dramatherapy is a specialized form of psychotherapy that harnesses the power of drama, theater, and creative expression to promote healing and personal growth.

A Holistic Approach to Healing

Unlike traditional talk therapy, dramatherapy engages the whole person – mind, body, and spirit – creating opportunities for profound healing and self-discovery.

Through the safe medium of creative play and storytelling, individuals can explore difficult emotions and develop greater self-awareness.

Evidence-Based Practice

Dramatherapy is recognized by the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC) and is used in hospitals, schools, and private practice settings worldwide.

A Compassionate Approach to Weight and Wellbeing

Many dieting programmes focus on quick, short-term weight loss. Our approach is different. We understand that some people may wish to lose weight, but our work focuses on helping you develop a healthy, sustainable, and compassionate relationship with food, eating, and your body.

As eating patterns stabilise and health behaviours improve, some people may notice gradual, natural changes in weight over time. However, we do not promise or promote weight-loss outcomes. Your emotional, psychological, and physical wellbeing is our priority.
