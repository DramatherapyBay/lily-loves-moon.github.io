What is Dramatherapy

A Holistic Approach to Healing

Unlike traditional talk therapy, dramatherapy engages the whole person – mind, body, and spirit, creating opportunities for profound healing and self-discovery. Through the safe medium of creative play and storytelling, individuals can explore difficult emotions and develop greater self-awareness.

This innovative therapeutic approach allows clients to safely explore their emotions, experiences, and relationships through techniques such as role-play, improvisation, storytelling, and creative movement.

Therapeutic Techniques

  • Role-Play & Character Exploration: Safely explore different perspectives and practice new behaviors through character work.
  • Creative Storytelling: Use narrative and metaphor to process experiences and discover new meanings.
  • Movement & Embodiment: Integrate body awareness and physical expression into the therapeutic process.
  • Improvisation & Spontaneity: Develop flexibility, creativity, and confidence through spontaneous expression.

Who Can Benefit?

  • Addiction recovery support
  • Anxiety and depression management
  • ADHD and autism spectrum support
  • Bereavement and grief processing
  • Cognitive impairments and dementia care
  • Emotionally Unstable Personality Disorder (EUPD)
  • Eating disorder treatment
  • Learning disability support
  • Life transition guidance
  • Personality disorder therapy
  • Trauma and PTSD healing

Professional Recognition

Dramatherapy is a regulated profession recognized by professional bodies including the North American Drama Therapy Association (NADTA) and British Association of Dramatherapists (BADth). It is evidence-based and internationally recognized.
