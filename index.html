Who Can Benefit?

Lily Yim Ching Lau

HCPC Registered Dramatherapist

Originally from Hong Kong, Lily brings a multicultural perspective to her practice as a British registered dramatherapist. She uses drama and creativity to support emotional and psychological well-being.

"I believe in the inherent capacity for healing that exists within every person."
